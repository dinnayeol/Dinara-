About us

Confectionery "WORLD OF SWEET TOOTH" was opened by a group of friends with vast experience. The experience of eating sweets.

As a result, we happily rented a room and assembled the optimal confectionery shop (graying a bit along the way). And for several months they had fun (no) with confectioners, creating a palette of flavors and sketching shapes.

We tried everything: blindfolded tastings to check the richness of taste, designing eclairs on paper and just enjoying the process. At that moment, when we began to run into the shop at night to steal a couple of cakes, we realized: well, that's it, you can start.
